3 Logo Usage Guidelines

The DO's

  • ✓ Always maintain the aspect ratio when scaling any JutJut logo.
  • ✓ Keep a minimum clear space of 15% of the logo's width around it.
  • ✓ Use the white logo variant on dark backgrounds to ensure high contrast.
  • ✓ Ensure the wordmark is always written in clean lowercase font when used as a logo.

The DON'Ts

  • ✗ Do not stretch, squeeze, or skew the logos or wordmarks.
  • ✗ Do not apply drop shadows, glows, or bevel effects to the minimal SVG design.
  • ✗ Do not alter the primary colors (Teal #0D9488 and Amber #F59E0B) of the logo.
  • ✗ Do not rotate the wordmark or place it on complex, busy backgrounds.
